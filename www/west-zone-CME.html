IAP Neonatology West Zone CME- 2015
"Neonatal Hemodynamics and Cardiology"
And Neonatal Simulation Workshop (NeoPedistars)
Kokilaben Hospital, Mumbai

Dates: 11th and 12th April 2015
CME: 12 th April 2015, Venue: Kokilaben Hospital, Mumbai

View Detail Schedule

IAP Neonatology Chapter in association with IAP and NNF Mumbai is organizing a one day CME on neonatal hemodynamics and Cardiology. It will focus on the practical issues and challenges faced in hemodynamics and management of the cardiac neonate.

Target audience: Neonatologists, practicing Pediatricians, neonatal fellows and Pediatric residents. It is designed with lectures, case studies, debates and interactive panel discussions.

CME: Fetal echocardiography, inotropes, neonatal shock, PDA, PPHN, approach to cyanotic newborn, functional echocardiography, surgical and catheter interventions in newborn.

Pre-CME workshop on Neonatal Simulation: This is a one day workshop where delegates have to perform on real life emergency scenarios in the NICU using simulation techniques. MMC credits applied.

The registration amount for the CME is Rs 1000/- and Rs. 1000/- for the Neonatal simulation workshop (draft in favor of “Mandke Foundation” to be sent before 30/3/2015). It should be sent along with complete personal details including the mobile numbers / email addresses, mailing address to:
